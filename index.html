My Promise我的承诺

Education should work for everyone. 为华生发声，照亮前行之路

01
Making Policy Accessible让政策通俗易懂

Policies like Extenuating Circumstances and AI Declaration exist to protect students — but only if they understand them. I will push for clearer communication, particularly for international students disadvantaged by complex academic language. 推动 EC 和 AI 政策的清晰沟通，重点关注国际学生。

02
Bridging the AI Gap弥合 AI 使用差距

The University has pushed AI forward, but how to use it responsibly remains unclear. I want to share my approach: using AI to manage time, preview material, and check work against marking schemes. I will be your student-University bridge. 负责任地利用 AI 进行时间管理和材料预览。

03
Supporting Quiet Struggles支持默默挣扎的学生

I took an interruption for two years. I understand the struggle. Once you've stood in the heavy rain, you want to share your umbrella with the next person who needs it. I will ensure proactive support reaches you before the breaking point. 我曾休学，我熬过来了。现在我想为你撑伞。
